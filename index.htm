Bikes and Why I Love Them


I always had a bike when I was growing up. I rode it everywhere, every day, all day. As I grew up and started driving, I lost any interest in riding. It wasn’t until I was in my early 30’s that I decided to get a road bike. I can’t explain what was different, but after that first ride cycling became a big part of my life.


It’s weird when you’re an adult to feel like you have a newfound sense of freedom, but that’s how I felt when I was riding. It was just me going as fast or as slow as I wanted and taking whatever course I felt like. I loved the feeling of accomplishment after a long ride to a new area.


Once I started mountain biking a year or so later, my love for bikes became a full-blown obsession. I set a 250 mile goal for 2020 and ended up riding well over 1000 miles that year. I’m almost convinced that I’m changing careers just so I have more money to spend on bike parts.
